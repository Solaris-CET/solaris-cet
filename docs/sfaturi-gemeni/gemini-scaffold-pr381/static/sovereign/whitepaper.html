Whitepaper

The Solaris CET project is a pioneering initiative that combines the power of the TON blockchain with AI-driven precision farming to scale rural innovation. Our mission is to create sustainable local growth in Puiești and beyond.

Technology

We leverage advanced AI models to optimize agricultural yields and manage local economic data. The RAV Protocol ensures multi-layered security for all Real-World Assets (RWA) integrated into the ecosystem.

Community
