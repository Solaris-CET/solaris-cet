## Important notice

This Service provides information and tooling related to crypto-assets. Crypto-assets are high risk. You can lose some or all of your funds.

Nothing on this website is investment advice, a recommendation, or an offer to buy/sell any asset.

## Key risks (non-exhaustive)

- **Volatility:** prices can change rapidly and unpredictably.
- **Liquidity risk:** you may not be able to sell at your desired time/price.
- **Smart contract risk:** contracts can contain bugs or be exploited.
- **Network risk:** congestion, downtime, forks, or protocol upgrades can affect transactions.
- **Counterparty risk:** third-party wallets, exchanges, bridges, or analytics tools may fail or be compromised.
- **Regulatory risk:** laws may change and restrict availability, trading, taxation, or reporting.
- **Operational risk:** lost private keys, phishing, and malware can lead to irreversible loss.

## Your responsibility

You are responsible for:

- verifying official contract addresses and explorers,
- understanding fees and slippage,
- securing your wallet and private keys,
- and complying with applicable laws.

If you are unsure, do not interact with crypto-assets and seek professional advice.
